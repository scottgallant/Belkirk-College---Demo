Barb is the Administrative Manager for Belkirk.

A UPEI alumni, she graduated in 2014 with a Masters of Business Administration and proceeded to work in project management within the university.

In 2016 she brought her experience in business management, communications, and innovation to Belkirk.

Barb is responsible for the daily administration of the Centre which includes managing budgets, creating project plans, and connecting with partners, stakeholders, and collaborators.
