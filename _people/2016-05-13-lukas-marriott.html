Lukas joined the CHCR in 2016 as a Research Assistant responsible for overseeing the daily operations of the CBT4CBT clinical trials. Lukas is the first point of contact for the trial participants and meets with active participants on a weekly basis.

He has a B.A. with Honours degree in Psychology from Acadia University as well as an Addictions Counselling Degree from NSCC.

Prior to joining the CHCR, Lukas worked as a Youth Worker for the Kings Regional Rehabilitation Centre in Waterville, Nova Scotia and as a Vocational Instructor for cognitively impaired adults in Windsor, Nova Scotia.
